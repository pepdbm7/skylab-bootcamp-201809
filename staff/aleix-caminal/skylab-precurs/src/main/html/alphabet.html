Memory Alphabet

Space: the final frontier. These are the voyages of the Star Trek universe. Your three-minute mission: to test your acknowledgement about strange new worlds, new life and new civilizations.

Welcome to Memory Alphabet, a game based on a famous television contest. Rules are simple: you will be given a letter and definition and you will have to answer the word that corresponds to it.

There are a few commands that you can use: answer 'pasapalabra' or leave the input empty to skip a word and answer it later, answer 'end' and the game will end but it will not count in the ranking.

You have three minutes to complete the questionary.
